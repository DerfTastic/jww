Failing at Solving Rubik's Cubes

January 27th, 2017

Sorry the title is a little bit deceiving, but the video is about me attempting to solve Rubik's Cubes (no durr). I finish the 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube in 45 seconds, the 5x5x5 Rubik's cube in 10:01 (not a time link), and I couldn't finish the 7x7x7 Rubik's cube. I had to look up videos on how to solve it, and even after that, I couldn't solve it. Enjoy!
